University of Centeral Florida

“The Joust” offers all UCF students an amazing opportunity to showcase their startup venture proposals, network with community leaders, and earn resources necessary to turn their visions into reality. Over $75,000 in cash and essential business services will be awarded to the best business proposals. Four finalists will also earn automatic admission to the Center for Entrepreneurial Leadership's new student venture accelerator program.

Finalists will compete on April 11th. This event is open to the Public. The final four contestants present their business ideas one last time for the public and the finals judges. Contest winners are announced at the awards ceremony that follows.

  • Location: Fairwinds Alumni Center
  • Contestant & Judge Check-in: 1:00 p.m.
  • Presentations: 1:30 – 4:00 p.m
  • Reception to follow: 4:15 – 5:15 p.m.

Helpful tips and more information can be found at: http://bit.ly/ucfjoust2014
