“The Joust” competition offers all UCF students an amazing opportunity to showcase their startup venture proposals, network with community leaders, and earn resources necessary to turn their visions into reality. Over $75,000 in cash and essential business services will be awarded to the best business proposals. Four finalists will also earn automatic admission to the Center for Entrepreneurial Leadership's new student venture accelerator program.
